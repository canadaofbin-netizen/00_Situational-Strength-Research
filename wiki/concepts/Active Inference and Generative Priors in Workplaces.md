# Active Inference and Generative Priors in Workplaces

This node synthesizes situational strength through the Free Energy Principle, replacing traditional psychological trait-activation with neuro-computational Active Inference.

## Core Mechanisms
1. **External Negentropy (Situational Strength)**: A "strong situation" is a low-entropy environment providing high-precision sensory priors. The environment absorbs the computational cost of resolving uncertainty. See [[Paper - Meyer 2009]].
2. **Internal Generative Priors (Personality Traits)**: Personality traits (e.g., Conscientiousness) are internal, high-precision generative priors. In weak (high-entropy) environments, employees must rely on their internal priors to minimize free energy and maintain goal-directed behavior. See [[Paper - Judge 2015]].
3. **Substitutability of Precision**: The fundamental law connecting Meyer and Judge: Internal precision and external precision are perfectly substitutable. If the environment is strong, internal traits are mathematically redundant.
